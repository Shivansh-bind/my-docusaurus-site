üéØ Course Objectives & Outcomes
Objectives
 * Introduce concepts of mathematical logic, sets, basic counting principles, relations, and functions.
 * Provide a basic idea of group theory and a detailed study of graph theory.
Course Outcomes (COs)
After completing this course, students will be able to:
 * CO 1: Understand different types of sets, their properties, and operations.
 * CO 2: Know the concept of basic counting principles, mathematical logics, and their properties.
 * CO 3: Understand basic concepts of group theory and perform a detailed study of trees, Boolean algebra, and graph theory.
