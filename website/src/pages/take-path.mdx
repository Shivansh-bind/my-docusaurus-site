## How to Use This Guide

This interactive roadmap helps you navigate your IT career journey. Simply:

1. **Choose a career path** that interests you
2. **Select a specialization** within that path
3. **Follow the levels** from 0 to 3 in order
4. **Check off topics** as you learn them
5. **Use the resources** provided for each level

Remember: Everyone starts at Level 0. There's no shame in being a beginner ‚Äì we all were once!

---

## Why These Roadmaps Work

Unlike random tutorials on YouTube or generic advice, these roadmaps:

- **Start from absolute zero** ‚Äì no prerequisites needed
- **Follow a logical order** ‚Äì each level builds on the previous
- **Include only free/cheap resources** ‚Äì no expensive courses required
- **Focus on job-ready skills** ‚Äì what companies actually want
- **Provide practice platforms** ‚Äì because learning by doing works best

---

## Important Notes

### For College Students

Your college curriculum gives you theoretical knowledge. These roadmaps give you practical skills. You need both to succeed in the IT industry.

### For Self-Learners

Don't try to learn everything at once. Pick one path, follow it consistently, and complete at least Level 1 before exploring other areas.

### For Career Switchers

IT welcomes people from all backgrounds. The key is showing you can do the work ‚Äì build projects, earn certifications, and demonstrate your skills.
